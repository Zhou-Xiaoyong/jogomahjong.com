Why play in relaxing mode?

Relaxing Mahjong is for those who want the pleasure of matching tiles without timer tension. Studies on casual games show that repetitive and calming activities, like matching pairs, help reduce anxiety and improve light focus — the famous "active rest".

Differences from classic mode

  • No timer: the clock only counts how long you played, without ending the game.
  • No penalty: making a mistake costs nothing. Wrong? Try another.
  • Unlimited hints: use as many times as you want, free of charge.
  • Automatic reshuffle: if no more moves remain, the board reorganizes itself.
  • Flatter layout: fewer layers, more free tiles from the start.

Ideal for

Work breaks, afternoons, before bed, or for those just starting to learn traditional Chinese Mahjong symbols without pressure. Each tile shows the Chinese symbol and English name, so you learn as you play.
