How to play Mahjong Slide

Mahjong Slide is a new and addictive mode, inspired by tile-matching games. The rule is super simple — but mastering it requires strategy and speed!

Basic rules

  • Drag sideways: click the ← and → buttons or use arrow keys.
  • Match equal tiles: when two tiles of the same type meet, they combine and disappear.
  • New tiles arrive: with each move, a new tile may appear in the row.
  • Don't let it fill up: if the row gets full and no matches are possible, the game ends.
  • Level up: every 10 pairs, the level increases and tiles arrive faster.

Tips to go far

  • Plan ahead: look at all tiles in the row before moving.
  • Create chain reactions: sometimes one move can cause multiple consecutive matches.
  • Keep the row organized: try to group similar types close together.
  • Don't rush: it's better to think a second than make a bad move.

Why this game is different

Unlike other mahjong modes on this site, Slide isn't about patience or long-term strategy — it's about quick reasoning and the ability to see matches. Perfect for those who enjoy games like 2048, match-3 and other tile-combining puzzles.
