How Mahjong Blitz works

Mahjong Blitz is the fastest and most exciting online mahjong mode. The rule is simple: you have 60 seconds to make as many pairs as possible. The board has 48 tiles (24 pairs) and, as you remove them, new tiles appear to keep the pace.

Basic rules

  • 60 seconds on the clock — every second counts!
  • Match equal pairs of tiles that are free (no tiles above and with one side free).
  • Each pair is worth 10 points. The faster you are, the more total points.
  • Board renews: when few tiles remain, new ones automatically appear.
  • Best score saved: your high score is saved in the browser.

Tips to score more

  • Look at the whole board: don't stay in one area — scan the entire board quickly.
  • Obvious pairs first: start with easy-to-see pairs to build rhythm.
  • Use hints sparingly: they help but waste precious time.
  • Don't hesitate: if you make a wrong click, keep going — time is the enemy.

Who this mode is for

Perfect for those who have only 1 or 2 minutes free and want a quick fun break. Also great for training reasoning speed and visual perception — skills that help in all other mahjong modes!
