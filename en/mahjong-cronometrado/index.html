How Timed Mahjong works

It's Mahjong Solitaire with a countdown timer. The board is the same as the classic mode, but now time is your adversary. The idea is simple: match equal and free tiles as fast as possible.

Time rules

  • You start with 5 minutes.
  • Each correct pair gives +2 seconds.
  • Each wrong pair takes -1 second.
  • Hint costs 3 seconds and shuffle costs 5 seconds.
  • If time runs out, the game ends.

Strategy to beat the clock

Don't click the first pair that appears. Save a few seconds to look at the entire board and plan a sequence of two or three moves. Start with upper layers and sides, which free more tiles. Save hints for when you're truly stuck — they're expensive in timed mode.

As time bonuses accumulate, a sequence of consecutive correct answers can recover lost time. The secret is maintaining rhythm without stumbling.
