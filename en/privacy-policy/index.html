Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Mahjong Game may handle information when you visit the site. The text was written in simple terms to make clear what happens during navigation.

Data we may collect

The game works without registration. We don't ask for name, email, phone or password to play. We may collect basic technical data, such as pages accessed, device type, browser, approximate country and visit time, always with the goal of improving the site.

Cookies and ads

The site may display ads in the future. Advertising partners, such as Google AdSense, may use cookies to measure performance, limit ad repetition and show more relevant advertising. When ads are activated, this page may be updated with additional information.

Access statistics

We may use traffic analysis tools to understand which pages are most useful, which devices are most used and where the site needs improvement. These statistics are aggregated and do not require registration.

Game and progress

The current version of the game does not create user accounts. Information such as time, moves and removed pairs are only used during the game in your browser.

External links

The site may contain links to other sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external pages.

Contact

If you need to talk about privacy, ads or site content, contact us through the channel informed in the future in the footer or contact page.
