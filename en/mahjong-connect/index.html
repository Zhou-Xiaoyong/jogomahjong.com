How to play Mahjong Connect

Mahjong Connect is a fun variation of the classic tile-matching game. Here the goal is also to find matching pairs, but with a special connection rule.

Rules

  1. Look for two tiles with the same symbol on the board.
  2. Check if it's possible to connect them with a straight line that makes at most two corners.
  3. The line cannot pass over other tiles.
  4. Click the two tiles to remove them.
  5. Continue until you clear the entire board.

Tips to win

Start with edge and corner tiles as they have fewer blocked paths. Pay attention to tiles that are "trapped" in the middle and try to free them first. Use the Hint button when you're stuck.
